  2. PENCIL


    3. "Pencil" offers a charming exploration of creativity through a simple yet captivating narrative. Its whimsical illustrations and thoughtful storytelling make it a delightful read for all

  3. A MILLION TO ONE


    4. "A Million to One" delivers a gripping heist set aboard the Titanic, blending historical fiction with high-stakes adventure. The diverse characters and their intricate plot make it a compelling read from start to finish.

  4. HIDE AND SEEK


    5. Hide and Seek by James Patterson is a thrilling novel that follows the story of Maggie Bradford, a famous singer and songwriter, as she navigates the dangerous world of celebrity and a series of harrowing events that challenge her resilience. The book masterfully combines suspense, drama, and a deep exploration of personal trauma, making it a gripping read from start to finish.

  5. OLD YOU


    6. "Old You" by Louise Voss is a gripping psychological thriller that explores themes of memory, identity, and trust. The story delves into the complexities of a marriage unraveling under the weight of hidden secrets and devastating truths.

  6. SUNSET NEVER WAITS.


    7. On the windswept Irish coast, Tara grieves her husband while a mysterious American named James arrives with secrets of his own. As a storm brews, both must confront their pasts to find a chance at love.

    8. On the windswept Irish coast, Tara grieves her husband while a mysterious American named James arrives with secrets of his own. As a storm brews, both must confront their pasts to find a chance at love.

  8. FLIGHT



    9. In the distant Aurora Galaxy, humanity finds itself caught between two warring immortal races. Daeson Starlore, a young pilot living a privileged life, stumbles upon a hidden truth that shatters his world. Now branded a traitor, Daeson flees on a perilous journey. He must unravel the secrets of the Starlore Legacy, an ancient prophecy that could hold the key to saving the galaxy. But time is running out.

  9. THE STAND



    10. A super flu wipes out most of humanity. Survivors flock to leaders - good Mother Abagail in Boulder, or dark Randall Flagg in Las Vegas. In this epic battle of good versus evil, humanity's fate hangs in the balance.

  10. THE MOON AND STARS



    11. "The Moon and Stars" by Jenna Warren takes readers on a celestial journey through lyrical prose, weaving tales of love and longing under the night sky. Warren's vivid imagery captures the essence of cosmic beauty, painting a dreamscape where emotions twinkle like distant stars. This book invites you to gaze upward, where each page reveals a new constellation of heartfelt stories, guiding readers through the mysteries of the human heart and the vastness of the universe.

  11. THE PERCY JACKSON



    12. Percy Jackson, a troubled young boy, discovers he's a demigod, son of Poseidon. At Camp Half-Blood, he trains for a quest to retrieve Zeus' stolen lightning bolt and prevent war among the gods.

  12. NEIL GAIMAN


    13. Neil Gaiman's "Norse Mythology" breathes fresh life into Viking tales. It follows the Norse gods, from Odin's ravens to Thor's mighty hammer, Weaving creation myths with thrilling adventures and ending with Ragnarok's prophesied doom,This captivating book makes Norse legends relevant and exciting for a modern audience.
